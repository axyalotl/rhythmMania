# rhythmMania
Project by Anya R, Esther O, Heidi W, and Abdul I 

Simple rhythm game with two difficulty modes.

How to play: 
In the menu screen, press E to play in Easy mode, and press H to play in Hard mode. Press M to open the user's manual, and press Q to quit the game.

Easy: 100 beats per minute song, less frequent beats.
Hard: 160 beats per minute song, more frequent beats.

Circles with keys will appear onscreen, with each corresponding to a beat for the song chosen. Press the correct arrow key as soon as the key circle is on top of the yellow-outlined reference circle. 
You earn points based on how close your keystrokes were from completely overlapping the reference circle; you can either score a 'Perfect,' a 'Good,' a 'Poor,' or a 'Miss.'
The points accumulate and assign you a grade based off how well you performed throughout the song, as follows: S (excellent), A, B, C, D, F (poor)
